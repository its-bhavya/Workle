HOW IT WORKS

1

Browse job postings or services on our platform. Students looking to post a job or freelancers seeking work can both explore available options.

2

If you're posting a job, provide details about the task and set the payment terms. Freelancers can apply to jobs that match their skills and interests.

3

Connect directly with students. Discuss the project scope, timelines, and payment. Once agreed upon, freelancers can start working on the job!
